== Demo script (~60 minutes)

=== Opening (5 min)

. Show link:https://showroom-showroom.%HUB_DOMAIN%/?USER_NAME=%USER_NAME%[Showroom] registration flow — audience gets `userN`.
. Open the architecture images above; state: **one OpenShift fleet**, AI is a shared service layer (MaaS), not a separate silo.
. Bill of Materials: every product is `charts/all/*` synced by Argo CD (`xref:index.adoc[Welcome]`).

=== Station 1 — Lightspeed + MCP (12 min)

**Message:** Natural-language operations for `%USER_NAME%` — scoped MCP tools, not unrestricted cluster admin.

. Developer Hub → link:https://developer-hub.%HUB_DOMAIN%/lightspeed[Lightspeed].
. Prompt: _"Which Argo CD applications are OutOfSync on this hub?"_
. Show MCP gateway link:https://mcp-gateway.%HUB_DOMAIN%/mcp[MCP Gateway] and catalog entity **workshop-mcp-gateway**.
. Terminal (hub): `oc get mcpserverregistration -n mcp-system`
. Deep dive: xref:24-mcp-gateway.adoc[Module 24 — MCP Gateway + Lightspeed]

TIP: Lightspeed uses **granite-3-2-8b-instruct** via MaaS OpenAI-compatible API. If chat fails, verify `llama-stack-secrets` in `developer-hub` and MaaS key post-install.

=== Station 2 — OpenShift AI + MaaS (12 min)

**Message:** `%USER_NAME%` gets project `ai-%USER_NAME%` — same models as the rest of the workshop.

. OpenShift AI dashboard → link:https://rhods-dashboard-redhat-ods-applications.%HUB_DOMAIN%[ODS Dashboard].
. Data Science Project **maas-workshop** → OpenShift AI Playground.
. Compare models: **Llama Scout 17B** (default chat) vs **DeepSeek R1 Distill Qwen 14B** (reasoning).
. Show per-user namespace: `oc get ns | grep ai-user`
. Deep dive: xref:22-openshift-ai.adoc[Module 22 — OpenShift AI Workshop]

image::22-openshift-ia-stack.png[OpenShift AI stack on hub,640]

=== Station 3 — NeuroFace multimodal (10 min)

**Message:** Edge vision (OVMS) + hub LLM (MaaS) — latency split for factory safety scenarios.

. Open link:https://neuroface.%HUB_DOMAIN%[NeuroFace] — enable webcam, detect object.
. Ask chat: _"What PPE is missing for this worker?"_
. Developer Hub → **neuroface-workshop** → Topology (OVMS + Route).
. Deep dive: xref:27-neuroface.adoc[Module 27 — NeuroFace]

=== Station 4 — DevSpaces + Continue on east (12 min)

**Message:** `%USER_NAME%` codes on the **same spoke** as Industrial Edge; Continue uses MaaS without exporting keys to laptops.

. Switch to east console or Showroom terminal: `east-login %USER_NAME%`
. Open link:https://devspaces.%EAST_DOMAIN%[DevSpaces east] → workspace for `%USER_NAME%`.
. Open Kaoto / Camel route; invoke Continue — model **deepseek-r1-distill-qwen-14b**.
. Explain: PostSync job `devspaces-continue-ai-sync` copies API key from `kairos-system/kairos-ai-credentials` into `{user}-devspaces/continue-ai-config`.
. Deep dive: xref:12-software-templates.adoc[Module 12 — Templates] + platform docs for DevSpaces.

=== Station 5 — Kairos + IE closure (9 min, optional)

**Message:** AI-assisted scaling closes the loop from Kafka telemetry to remediation policies.

. link:https://kairos-console-kairos-system.%HUB_DOMAIN%[Kairos Console] — show east/west sensor SmartScalingPolicy mirrors.
. link:https://industrial-edge.%HUB_DOMAIN%[Industrial Edge dashboard] — live metrics while NeuroFace / alerts narrative plays.
. image::14-kairos-ia-agents.png[Kairos agents and hub reporting,640]
. Deep dive: xref:14-kairos-scaling.adoc[Module 14 — Kairos scaling]

=== Close (5 min)

. Recap xref:28-ai-end-user-apps.adoc[Module 28 — AI in end-user apps]: operators see AI where they work.
. Tease Vault/ESO path for MaaS keys (post-install, rollout required).
. Q&A — point advanced learners to modules 23–26 for gateway, RAG, and predictive text.

== Show and Tell checklist

Facilitator checklist before the room enters:

* [ ] `oc get managedclusters` — east/west **Available**
* [ ] `export MAAS_KEY_LLAMA=…` → `bash scripts/apply-maas-secrets.sh` (or RHDP `litemaas.apiKey`)
* [ ] `oc get secret neuroface-maas-api-key -n neuroface` — not `CHANGEME-inject-via-RHDP`
* [ ] `curl -sk -o /dev/null -w '%{http_code}' https://developer-hub.%HUB_DOMAIN%/lightspeed` → 2xx
* [ ] NeuroFace chat POST → **200** (not **401**)
* [ ] `curl -sk -o /dev/null -w '%{http_code}' https://workshop-apis.%HUB_DOMAIN%/httpbin/get` → **401** (protected route)
* [ ] `curl -sk -o /dev/null -w '%{http_code}' https://vault-vault.%HUB_DOMAIN%/ui/` → **200**
* [ ] Registration tested with a fresh `userN`
* [ ] Webcam permission tested for NeuroFace (HTTPS required)
* [ ] Day-2 applied: `bash scripts/apply-post-install-day2.sh` (or individual apply-* scripts)
